ClipReplay Terms of Service

Last updated: May 8, 2026

ClipReplay is a private creator workflow tool for preparing short-form video posts, adapting captions, and publishing authorized content to connected social accounts.

Authorized use

You may only use ClipReplay with accounts you own or are authorized to manage. You are responsible for ensuring that every uploaded video, caption, hashtag, and post complies with the rules of each destination platform.

Content ownership

You retain ownership of your uploaded content. ClipReplay does not claim ownership of videos, captions, drafts, schedules, or channel data.

Platform integrations

When you connect third-party services, ClipReplay uses the permissions you grant only to prepare, upload, and publish content on your behalf. You can revoke access from each platform's account settings.

Limitations

ClipReplay is provided as a local workflow tool without warranties. Publishing can fail because of platform review, API limits, account restrictions, connectivity, or invalid media.

Contact

For questions about this tool, contact the ClipReplay operator who provided access to your workspace.
