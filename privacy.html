ClipRelay Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 13, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how ClipRelay processes information when you upload videos, connect social accounts, publish content, and review audience feedback through the service.

1. Information we collect

ClipRelay may collect and process uploaded media files, captions, draft content, scheduling data, platform-specific post metadata, connected account names, account identifiers, comment or mention data from supported platforms, and technical logs needed to operate the workflow.

2. Authorization and account data

When you connect a platform account, ClipRelay may store access tokens, refresh tokens, usernames, profile identifiers, and related authorization metadata required to maintain the connection and perform the actions you request.

3. How we use information

ClipRelay uses information to upload media, generate or rewrite copy, save drafts, display connected accounts, publish posts, retrieve supported feedback items, and send replies where the connected platform permits in-app responses.

4. AI-assisted features

If you use AI copy or reply assistance, relevant prompt text and context may be sent to the configured AI provider so the requested text can be generated. ClipRelay uses that content only to complete the requested drafting action.

5. Third-party services and platforms

When you publish or retrieve feedback through YouTube, Instagram, TikTok, X, or other supported services, ClipRelay shares the content and metadata required to complete the action through each platform’s official API or authorization flow.

6. Storage and retention

Workflow data may be stored locally by the operator and, when deployed on hosted infrastructure, on the configured hosting environment. Data is retained only as long as needed to provide the workflow or until the operator deletes files, tasks, drafts, or channel connections.

7. Security

ClipRelay uses reasonable measures to protect stored workflow data and connected account credentials. However, no transmission or storage system is guaranteed to be completely secure, and you should revoke access immediately if you suspect unauthorized use.

8. Your choices

You may disconnect platforms, delete drafts or tasks, remove uploaded media, and revoke third-party access from the connected platform’s own account settings.

9. Children’s privacy

ClipRelay is not intended for children under the age required by applicable law to authorize connected publishing accounts. Do not use the service to process personal information from children in a way that violates platform or legal requirements.

10. Updates and contact

ClipRelay may update this Privacy Policy as the service changes. For privacy or data handling questions, visit the public support channel at ClipRelay support.
