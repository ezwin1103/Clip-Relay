ClipRelay

Upload once, tailor per platform, and publish with one workspace.

ClipRelay is a web-based creator operations tool for preparing short-form video releases, adapting channel-specific copy, publishing to connected social accounts, and reviewing audience feedback in one place.

Open the app workspace View TikTok review notes

What ClipRelay does

  • Upload one original short-form video and keep the source asset organized.
  • Write a master caption, then tailor or rewrite text for each destination platform.
  • Connect your own social accounts and explicitly publish authorized content.
  • Review publish history, draft content, and a shared audience feedback inbox.

How the workflow works

ClipRelay is designed for a single release workflow: source asset, platform-native copy, connected destinations, then publish or schedule.

1. Prepare the asset

Upload one source video, review metadata, and keep reusable uploads in the library without recreating each release from scratch.

2. Localize the copy

Draft a master caption, generate per-platform copy, rewrite with AI assistance, and adjust each destination before publishing.

3. Publish or schedule

Send the release to connected channels, track publish status, and review incoming audience comments from supported platforms.
